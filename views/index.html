Can you park there?

Our maps provide real time parking information with user submited "Sygns"

Use our maps to find parking information in your area, or search other areas of interest.

Never park in a restricted space again, our maps will mark "No Parking" zones red.

Read and update "Sygn" information remotely using our application!

"Sygns" definition: real life parking signs logged digitally by our users to assist and inform others of public parking restrictions. Our goal is to cover the entire digital map of los angeles in sygns!

Our layers are color coded to follow general parking guidelines: green markers designate areas that are open for public parking, red for currently restricted, yellow for loading zones, etc. The street parking markers will change dynamically based on the current time and day!
