SYGNs - Help

Step 1:
Sign into your 'Home' page to view a map of parking restrictions.

Step 2:
View restrictions in your area, or tap the map to start a new restriction. Tap again to end the parking restriction (AKA "Sygn").

Step 3:
Follow the window prompt and complete the form to help us and other users avoid restricted parking zones.
